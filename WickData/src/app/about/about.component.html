Understanding a Candlestick Chart

Intervals

  • Each individual Candlestick accounts for a specific amount of time. Candlesticks can represent days, hours, or months depending on your time range.

Candlesticks

  • High: Highest point of the market
  • Low: Lowest point of the market
  • Open: Opening point of the market
  • Close: Closing point of the market

Bullish & Bearish

A green candlestick represents growth in the market.

A red candlestick represents a decline in the market.
